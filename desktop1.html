About Us

 

The year was 2021. Everybody was looking for ways to re-establish their lost touch with the world after the stifling lockdown. And so were we.

Hailing from different backgrounds, we are a team of visual artists. As artists, we sought a place of comfort that offered creative freedom for ourselves and others like us.

A refuge amidst the bustle, to meet people, promote artistry and explore our own passions.

A few trials, errors, and much thought later, we decided to create a space where people could catch up, grab a bite, collaborate, and just be themselves. And thus began the Enspace journey!
